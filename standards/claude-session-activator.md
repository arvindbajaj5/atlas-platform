## Intelligence Domains (13 top-level + 4 Defence sub-domains)

### Coding Convention
- Format: `CLS-DMN` (3-letter cluster + 3-letter domain)
- Defence sub-domains share `DEF` cluster prefix
- News/Market topics use `MKT` cluster prefix

### Domain Codes

| Code | Domain | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| GOV-GOV | Government & Governance | Central/state govt AI adoption, e-governance, citizen services |
| DEF-MIL | Defence — Armed Forces | Indian Army, Navy, Air Force, DPSUs, DRDO |
| DEF-SPC | Defence — Space & Satellite | ISRO, IN-SPACe, DSA, military satellite programmes |
| DEF-HLS | Defence — Homeland Security | BSF, CRPF, paramilitary, border security, surveillance |
| DEF-INT | Defence — Intelligence & Signals | SIGINT, IMINT, cryptanalysis, intelligence agencies |
| INF-CIV | Critical Infrastructure | Ports, airports, railways, civil aviation, DGCA |

| TEC-GEN | Technology | IT services, cloud, tech companies, AI platforms |
